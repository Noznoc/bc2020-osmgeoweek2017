Building Canada 2020 OSMGeoWeek Mapathons

From November 10 - 20, 2017, Canadian colleges and universities participated in Building Canada 2020 (BC2020) mapathons for OSMGeoWeek! BC2020 is a community-led project to map all building footprints by 2020 on OpenStreetMap (OSM). Click here to learn more about the project. The mapathons educated students how to map buildings with OSM tools. Participants armchaired mapped Canadian regions that are not represented in OSM and do not have a open building footprint dataset that can be imported. You can read more about the BC2020 mapathons here.

5,762 buildings were added to OSM by 201 contributors
